WHAT IS THE
ABILITY EXPERIENCE?

❤

Founded in 1977, The Ability Experience is Pi Kappa Phi's national philanthropy that serves people with disabilities. Through events like War of the Roses, we raise funds to support programs that build inclusive communities and empower individuals with disabilities to reach their full potential.

Since 1987, Journey of Hope cyclists have pedaled across the country to spread a message of acceptance and understanding for people with disabilities. All proceeds from War of the Roses go directly to supporting these life-changing programs.
